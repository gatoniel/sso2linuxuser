Steps to registration

  1. Click register bellow
  2. You will be redirected to your identity provider (ILIAS)
  3. Login with your username and password
  4. You will be prompted to enter a new password for this workstation
  5. Now you can login at the workstation

If you have registered already, you cannot register again. Procceed with the login to JupyterHub.
